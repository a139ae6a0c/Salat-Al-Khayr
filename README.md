# Salat Al-Khayr (Alpha)

![Salat Al-Khayr Logo](logo.png)

**Salat Al-Khayr** is a desktop prayer time application built in C#. It sits cleanly on your desktop, providing accurate prayer times, a real-time countdown to the next Salah, and automated Adhan playback. 

**Current Status:** ALPHA
*Note: This software is currently in Alpha. Features may change, and you may encounter minor bugs.*

* **Desktop Widget:** A sleek, unobtrusive desktop overlay showing the times for Fajr, Dhuhr, 'Asr, Maghrib, and 'Ishaa.
* **Live Countdown:** Displays a real-time countdown to the upcoming prayer and highlights it on the interface.
* **Mawaqit Integration:** Sync your prayer times directly with your local Masjid by pasting your custom Mawaqit URL.
* **Custom Adhan Audio:** Upload your own `.mp3` files to customize the call to prayer.
* **Volume Control:** Built-in volume slider to adjust the Adhan playback to your preference.

To configure the application, open the Settings menu (via the gear icon):
1. **Mawaqit URL:** Paste the specific Mawaqit URL for your local mosque (e.g., `https://mawaqit.net/en/...`).

3. **Adhan Audio:** Select a default Adhan or click **Upload MP3** to use a custom file.
4. Click **Apply Settings** to save.
